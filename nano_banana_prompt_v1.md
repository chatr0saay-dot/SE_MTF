# Figure Generation Prompt: Integrated Research Workflow

## Target Style
- **Visual Style:** Professional scientific illustration (Nature/Cell/BioRender inspired)
- **Design:** Minimalist, clean, vector-style, isometric perspective
- **Color Palette:** Professional blues, grays, and subtle accent colors (muted tones)
- **Background:** Solid white

## Workflow Steps (Left to Right)
1. **Biochemistry:** - Icons: A multi-channel pipette, a 96-well plate with colorful liquid, and a schematic of a protein or cell culture.
2. **Microscopy Design:** - Icons: A simplified but accurate optical path, including a laser source (light beam), an objective lens, and a sample stage.
3. **Image Processing:** - Icons: A computer monitor displaying a high-resolution fluorescence micrograph (cell structures) with an overlay of a neural network or segmentation heatmap.

## Technical Constraints
- No garbled or messy text.
- High resolution, sharp lines, consistent lighting.
- Clean transitions between steps using subtle arrows or layout flow.
